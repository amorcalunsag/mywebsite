My Adventures

Four kinds of places that keep calling me back—each with its own lesson.

Mountain Adventure

Everything feels lighter and easier the moment I step into the mountain hills and plains. It feels calm and peaceful, and my mind reaches the highest point of clamness and relaxation.This part of nature reminds me to take pause for a while to rest. It also reminds me that I can come here whenever I need comfort.

Beach Adventure

Even a simple walk to the seashore has a great impact on me. Because it makes me feel lively again after becoming drained from school activities. The limitless ocean, the peaceful surroundings, the small waves, and the cold breeze help me feel better and relax.

Camping Experience

Everytime i say, I need space, they difinitely take me to a place where the surroundings are green. The moment we gather with our friends in different way, away from gadgets and the busy world. Every minutes brings chill, joy and quality time.

Nature Exploration

A quiet walk under tall tress and unclear paths, where the noise of leaves dancing in the wind, the chirping of birds, and the sound of crickets can be heard. It doesn't feel lonesome; it feels blessed as I noticed everthing. That everywhere in this world is abundant with living creatures. It feels different when you appreciate them.
